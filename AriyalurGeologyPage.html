Introduction to a Fossil Sites in Ariyalur

The Cretaceous rocks are exposed in Ariyalur area in South India.
These rocks formations are grouped into four groups namely, the Niniyur,
Ariyalur, Trichinopoly, and Uttatur, groups.

Outcrops of these groups are seen in Ariyalur and in its surounding areas.
In these outcrops we find fossils like Ammonites, Bivalues, Gastropods,
Echinoids, Plant fossils, etc.
