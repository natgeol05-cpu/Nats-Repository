Industries in Ariyalur

The major industries relevent to Geologist are mainly Limestone Quaries and Cement Factories.

Limestone Quarries in Tamilnadu
Limestone quarries are present in several districts in Tamil Nadu, with notable reserves
in areas like Salem, Tiruchirappalli, Ariyalur, Perambalur, and Thoothukudi.
Specifically, fossiliferous limestone, a major type of limestone, is found in Tiruchirappalli,
Ariyalur, Perambalur, and Thoothukudi districts.

The total reserve of fossiliferous limestone is estimated at 670 million tonnes.

Limestone quarries in Ariyalur:

• Kallankurichi Limestone Kankar Quarry:A public consultation meeting was held for the expansion of this quarry.

• Pudupalayam village: Residents and environmentalists opposed the proposed limestone mining by TNCC, citing potential environmental impacts.

• Ramco Simmons Limited: This company owns and operates limestone mines in the area, using both conventional and non-conventional mining methods.

• Alathiyur block: This block is part of the limestone belt in Sendurai Taluk and is currently being explored for limestone resources.

• Periakurichi Block: A proposal for preliminary exploration of limestone in this block was submitted to the National Mineral Exploration Trust.

• Periapurichi area: Dalmia Cement Bharat Limited holds a mining lease in this area, with the limestone deposit being of sedimentary origin.

• Ariyalur and Sendurai Taluks: Limestone of cement-grade quality has been found in these areas.

Cement Production in TN company wise
In Tamil Nadu, the major cement producers include Tamil Nadu Cements Corporation Ltd, Zuari Cement Ltd,
UltraTech Cement Ltd, Dhandapani Cements (P) Ltd, and The India Cements Ltd, among others.
These companies operate multiple plants across the state, including locations in Ariyalur, Ponneri,
and other areas. Tamil Nadu Cements Corporation Ltd (TANCEM) has plants in Alangulam and Ariyalur.
The major Cement Producers :

• Tamil Nadu Cements Corporation Ltd: Operates plants in Ariyalur and Alangulam, producing Ordinary Portland Cement and Portland Pozzolana Cement.
• Zuari Cement Ltd: Has a plant in Athipattu, producing Portland Cement (PPC) and Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC).
• UltraTech Cement Ltd: Operates plants in Reddipalayam and Arakkonam, producing Ordinary Portland Cement and Portland Pozzolana Cement.
• Dhandapani Cements (P) Ltd: Has a plant in Thathamangalam, producing OPC and PPC.
• The India Cements Ltd: Operates a plant in Narnamalpuram, producing cement.
• Chettinad Cement Corporation: Has multiple plants in Tamil Nadu, including Ariyalur, Karikkali, and Puliyur.

Tamil Nadu Cement Corporation Limited (TANCEM) has 2 cement plants: Alangulam Cement Works and Ariyalur Cement Works. Alangulam Cement Works: Location – Alangulam, Virudhunagar, Virudhunagar District.

Cement manufacturing Process
Cement making is a multi-step process that begins with raw materials like limestone and clay being mined and crushed, then mixed and heated in a kiln to form clinker.
The clinker is then ground into a fine powder and combined with gypsum
to create the final cement product.
