Description

Name: Ptilophyllum
Formation: Karai and Terani Formations
Locality: Bot5h at Karai and Terani
Classification: Order Bennettitales Family Williamsoniaceae Genus Ptilophyllum,
Age: Late Triassic (230mya)

This is Early Cretaceous fossil Ptilophyllum leaf collected from clay outcropes at Karai
and Terani in Karai Formation and also found Terani Formation of Uttatur Group.

Ptillophyllum fossils date back to at least Late Triassic (230 mya), but may have
originated much earlier, possibly in the Permian or
Carboniferrous periods (about 300 mya).

Some Ptilophyllum fossils have also been found in the Early Cretaceous,
in Karai and Terani Formations of Uttatur Group.

Ptilophyllum is an extinct form genus of leaves belonging to
extinct seed plane order Bennettitales.

Ptilophyllum is characterised by small, closely set or imbricating pinnacles,
sub-oppositely fully attached to the rachis.
